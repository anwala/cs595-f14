We here at The Ken Reedy Show worship in the church of professional wrestling and invite you to do the same. We plan on taking over the world "One Championship Belt at a Time".


In addition to our brilliantly witty host we have assembled the funniest, zaniest group of misfits and miscreants since the Oddities: co-host "Diamond Dave" Rosenbluth, producer and resident "Diva" Michele De G and weekly show blogger Tony "V". Of course we can't forget the cast of characters that call in every week!

Together we discuss, dissect, dismantle and defend the world of professional wrestling as you know and love it today.
