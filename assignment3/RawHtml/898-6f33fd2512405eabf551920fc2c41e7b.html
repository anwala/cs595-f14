This page (and station) is under construction! We are "on the air" over the Internet!

Please forgive the occasional audio level changes, dropouts, etc. as we are very much still in testing mode!

GraceRadio FM106.1, Lord willing, will be a low power FM station (LPFM) serving the city of Modesto, California, as well as the world via the Internet.

We will feature sound Biblical teaching, from local pastors as well as nationally and even internationally known pastors and teachers.

We will also broadcast a mix of soothing and uplifting music including hymns, psalms, classical music, some softer Christian Contemporary music... and the occasional curveball...

We will air other edifying features, news, and short stories, all to glorify our God and to proclaim our Lord Jesus Christ as Lord and Savior!
