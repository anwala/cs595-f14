Today, Kendra Scott is a leading fashion accessories brand sold at premiere retailers including Neiman Marcus, Nordstrom, Bloomingdale’s and over 600 specialty boutiques worldwide. The brand is known for its kaleidoscope of beautiful accessories as well as the unique customizeable Color Bar experience, available in stores and online. It has been worn by the likes of Jessica Alba, Mindy Kaling, Brooklyn Decker and Selena Gomez. As the brand continues to grow, the company remains true to its founding philosophy of “Family, Fashion, Philanthropy.” I was introduced to Kendra Scott’s gorgeous collection during the New York Fashion Week Reward Style party where we were gifted the breathtaking Naomi Double Ring in Platinum Drusy and Ivy Bracelet in Gold or Silver. I am so obsessed with both pieces, they are unique statement looks and are incredible quality, gold plated brass. They are my new go to statement pieces!
